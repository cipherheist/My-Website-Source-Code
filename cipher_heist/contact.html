We provide SHA-256 checksums and PGP signatures for all downloadable tools. These can be found on each tool's download page.

To verify a PGP signature:

  1. Import our public key: gpg --recv-keys 0xABCDEF123456
  2. Verify the signature: gpg --verify toolname.sig toolname.exe

Always verify downloads before executing them, especially security tools.
