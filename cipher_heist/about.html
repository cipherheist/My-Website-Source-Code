About Cipher Heist

Democratizing cybersecurity through open-source tools and education

Our Mission

At Cipher Heist, we believe cybersecurity knowledge should be accessible to everyone. Founded in 2020, we develop free, open-source tools that help developers, ethical hackers, and security professionals test systems, automate security workflows, and learn through practical experience.

Our community-driven approach ensures our tools remain transparent, reliable, and effective against evolving digital threats. We're committed to making security simple without compromising on quality or performance.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Accessibility

We break down barriers to cybersecurity knowledge with free tools and resources available to all skill levels.

Transparency

All our tools are open-source with clear documentation so users can verify and understand how they work.

Community

We foster collaboration through open development, feedback loops, and shared learning experiences.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our development

2020 - Foundation

Launched with our first tool, CleanWindowsPro, to help users remove bloatware and secure Windows systems.

2021 - Community Growth

Reached 1,000 GitHub stars and established our Discord community for security enthusiasts.

2022 - Tool Expansion

Released HackTypeFX and PortGuard, expanding our toolkit to include educational and network security utilities.

2023 - Website Launch

Debuted our web platform with interactive vulnerability scanner and comprehensive resource library.
